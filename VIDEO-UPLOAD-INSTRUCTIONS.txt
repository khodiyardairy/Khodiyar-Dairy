========================================================================
             KHODIYAR DAIRY & PRODUCTS - VIDEO UPLOAD INSTRUCTIONS
========================================================================

To complete the premium video hero implementation on your website, you must 
manually upload your video and fallback poster image to the following paths 
in your project:

1. VIDEO FILE:
   - Target location in project: public/videos/khodiyar-dairy-hero.mp4
   - Content: A beautiful, lightweight MP4 video showing premium dairy and 
     sweets visuals (such as fresh milk pouring, penda, sweets being 
     prepared, ghee, dry fruits, and elegant product close-ups).
   - Format: MP4 (.mp4) only.
   - Recommended settings: Highly compressed (aim for under 3MB) to keep 
     the website blazing fast on mobile networks.

2. POSTER/FALLBACK IMAGE:
   - Target location in project: public/images/hero-poster.jpeg
   - Content: A beautiful static high-quality snapshot representing your 
     dairy brand to display while the video is loading, on slow connections, 
     reduced-motion users, or if the video cannot load.
   - Format: JPEG (.jpeg / .jpg).

------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHY MANUALLY?
------------------------------------------------------------------------
To respect the high performance of your free Vercel hosting, we do not auto-generate, 
convert, or bundle heavy mock video files into your codebase. You retain absolute 
control over the final video quality, compression, and visual presentation!

The website has been fully coded with modern network-aware performance logic:
- Automatically detects and disables autoplay on slow mobile connections (2G, 3G).
- Automatically respects users with "Data Saver" or "Reduced Motion" preferences.
- Gracefully displays your poster image if any load errors or browser restrictions occur.
